Building AI that actually ships

I'm Ahsan Ali, an AI Engineer based in Farooq Abad, Pakistan, with a focus on taking machine learning from notebook to production. My work sits at the intersection of research and engineering — where a well-tuned model meets a system that can serve it reliably at scale.

My current work centers on Large Language Models: fine-tuning open-source models for domain-specific tasks, building Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipelines, and designing the evaluation frameworks that tell you when a model is actually ready to deploy — not just when it looks good on a benchmark.

What drives me is the gap between "it works in the demo" and "it works in production." Bridging that gap — with robust MLOps, thoughtful architecture, and obsessive attention to inference cost — is where I spend most of my time.
